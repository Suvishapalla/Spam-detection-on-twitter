# Spam-detection-on-twitter


Social networking platforms have become popular hubs for internet users to connect and engage. Twitter, among the most widely used platforms, facilitates news consumption, idea sharing, discussions on social issues, and staying in touch with loved ones. Unfortunately, its immense popularity has attracted spammers. Existing machine learning-based spam detection methods often fall short due to spammers' evolving tactics to evade detection. This paper introduces an alternative approach, employing the Multinomial Naive Bayes classifier, which incorporates tweet content and user metadata for spam detection.
